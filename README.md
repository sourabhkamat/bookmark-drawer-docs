[Bookmark Drawer](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/bookmark-drawer/nglmebfafdbfoegegpeohmaokdebjlec?authuser=0&hl=en)

A fast, native-feeling app drawer that replaces Chrome's default bookmark experience.

Bookmark Drawer completely transforms how you interact with your saved websites. Instead of hunting through endless dropdown menus or cluttered text lists, this extension turns your bookmarks into a beautiful, native-feeling "App Drawer" right inside your browser.

### Features
* **Visual Grid Layout:** View your bookmarks as large, easy-to-click app icons. 
* **Drag and Drop:** Effortlessly drag icons to rearrange them, or drop them on top of each other to instantly create categorized folders. 
* **High-Res Icons:** Automatically fetches beautiful, high-quality logos for your saved websites. 
* **Deep Customization:** Fully tailor your experience with Light/Dark modes, custom background colors, adjustable grid sizes, and custom icon shapes (Squircle, Rounded, Circle). 
* **Custom Uploads:** Don't like a website's default icon? Upload your own custom images directly into the drawer! 
* **Privacy First:** We aggressively strip tracking parameters and private paths from your URLs before fetching missing icons, ensuring your browsing habits stay private.
