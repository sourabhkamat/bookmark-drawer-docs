# Privacy Policy for Bookmark Drawer

At Bookmark Drawer, we believe your browsing data is yours alone. We've designed this extension from the ground up to respect your privacy and keep your information safe on your own device.

## How we use your bookmarks
To work its magic, the extension needs permission to read your bookmarks. We only use this permission to display your bookmarks beautifully on your screen. Your bookmark data is processed locally and never leaves your computer.

## How we get your high-res icons (and why it's secure)
To make your app drawer look fantastic, we use a strict, privacy-first system to source your icons:
1. **Custom & Built-in Icons:** We first check if you've uploaded a custom icon
2. **Public Websites:** If we need to fetch an icon for a public website, we securely ask Google or DuckDuckGo's favicon services. However, we go the extra mile for your privacy: we strictly strip away your private URLs before fetching. If you bookmark a private document (like `docs.company.com/secret-file-123`), we only send the main website name (`docs.company.com`) to get the icon. Your private paths and document IDs are never leaked to the internet.
3. **Private & Local Networks:** We automatically detect private or local network addresses (like `localhost`, `192.168.x.x`, or router portals). For these, we **never** contact external services. Instead, we securely rely on Chrome's native, internal favicon cache.

## Where your settings live
We don't have any servers, and we don't want your data. We don't collect, track, or sell your information. Any custom settings or personal icons you upload are saved directly to your browser's local hard drive.

## Standard Legal Protection
Because this is a free tool made for the community, it is provided "as is". While we've built it carefully, the developer cannot be held legally liable for any unexpected browser glitches or data loss. By using the extension, you agree to use it at your own risk.
